# staffroom — Brand Narrative

## Essence

Teacher-powered platform that helps educators discover real, trustworthy
workplaces and helps schools build credibility through authentic teacher
experiences.

## Vision

A world where every school is a great place for teachers to work and grow.

## Mission

Empower teachers to make informed career choices and help schools strengthen
trust through real insights, authentic teacher experiences, and meaningful
recognition of great workplaces.

## Positioning

India's first teacher-powered platform for real insights into school
workplaces, helping great teachers find great schools, and great schools
earn trust through transparency.

## Core Promise

Real teacher insights you can trust.

## Tagline

Where great teachers meet great schools.

## Signature Analogy

Tripadvisor for teachers — helping you find the best schools to work in,
based on real experiences.
Tripadvisor for schools — where great workplaces get discovered and trusted.

(Functional model: Glassdoor. Brand positioning: Tripadvisor. Use the
Tripadvisor analogy in external communication — positive, discovery-oriented,
not complaint-driven.)

## Values

**Transparency** — Make the realities of school workplaces clear and
accessible. Explain verification, anonymity, and data sources clearly.
Never hide reviews unless they violate community guidelines.

**Recognition** — Celebrate and elevate schools that create great environments
for teachers. Use Gold sparingly — only for real excellence backed by review
volume.

**Collaboration** — Enable constructive dialogue that helps teachers and
schools grow together. Frame reviews as input, not verdicts.

## Personality

**Empathetic** — Acknowledge feelings and experiences respectfully. Warm,
supportive language — never clinical or cold.

**Credible** — Prioritise real teacher insights over unverified claims.
Maintain neutrality. Use precise, grounded language.

**Grounded** — Speak plainly and respectfully. Avoid lofty promises.
Understand the constraints schools operate within.

**Optimistic** — Frame improvement as possible and accessible. Celebrate
small wins and incremental progress.

## Voice System

**Teacher Voice** — Warm, constructive, empowering.
> "Find workplaces where you can grow and share your experience to help
> others make informed choices."

**Institution Voice** — Collaborative, credible, respectful.
> "Recognizing schools that invest in teacher well-being and growth."

**Voice Stance** — Celebrate excellence and invite improvement. Never accuse.

## Key Design Principles

These govern how the brand behaves across all output:

- Teacher-powered, not teacher-only
- Collaborative, not adversarial
- Evidence-backed, not anecdotal
- Positive, not promotional
- Grounded, not pessimistic
- Celebration-first, not complaint-first

## Emotional Outcome

What teachers and schools should feel after any staffroom interaction:
> "I feel informed, respected, and confident in my choices — part of a
> community where teachers and schools grow through transparency, trust,
> and shared recognition."
