# Video Production Brief Template

Use this template for any staffroom video asset — YouTube Short, Instagram
Reel, Meta video ad, or YouTube Long-form episode. A completed brief can be
handed to an AI video generation agent OR a human creator with no additional
context needed.

**BEFORE FILLING:** Read `brand-custodian/references/visual-identity.md` for
brand colours and typography. Read `content-strategy/references/script-style-guide.md`
for hook types and beat structure.

---

**Pillar:** [content pillar — one of the 5 from content-pillars.md]
**Hook type:** [Authority / Data / Validation]
**Language:** [English / Hinglish — specify mix]

**Beat 4 — Escalation (31–50s):**
[Climax or surprising insight. Future pacing if applicable.]
